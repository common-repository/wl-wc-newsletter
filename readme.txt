== Description ==

WL Newsletter for WooCommerce allows you to enable newsletter subscription for your visitors and reward them with WooCommerce discount coupons. This plugin can be a very useful if you are looking to grow your sales revenue for your e-commerce store.

With WL Newsletter for WooCommerce you can manage & send newsletters to your subscribers from within the Wordpress dashboard. It provides one stop solution for all your newsletter campaign needs. No more sending one email to every recipients. Now you can write a single email and our plugin will automatically personalize it for every recipients. Continue reading to learn about more feature.

== Dependencies ==
Before you install this plugin make sure you have the [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) & [Easy WP SMTP](https://wordpress.org/plugins/easy-wp-smtp/) plugins installed, activated & set up on your website.

= WL Newsletter for WooCommerce Features =

* Allow visitors to your website to subscribe to your newsletters from the my-account or checkout pages.
* Reward them with WooCommerce discount coupon for subscribing or disable this feature.
* Use custom email from & reply-to email headers for your welcome & newsletter emails.
* Show your own terms of subscription to the visitors on the subscription form.
* Automatically categorize subscribers into Guest, Customer & Member.
* Manage subscribers.
* Create newsletter and send it immediately or save it to send later.
* View details of sent newsletter including the subscribers to whom the newsletter was successfully sent.
* Edit / Delete newsletters that are not sent.
* Duplicate newsletters.
* Send personalized newsletter to a particular subscriber, subscribers belonging to any particular mailing list or all subscribers in one click.

Once you have installed the plugin there are some options that you need to configure in the plugin setttings (go to `WL Newsletter->settings` from your WordPress Dashboard).

The settings page consists of the following options:

* **Enable Newsletter Subscription:** Whether or not to allow visitors to subscribe to your newsletter while making an order or signing up for an account.
* **Enable Subscription Coupon Offer:** Whether or not to send subscription coupon to new subscribers?
* **Discount Coupon Type (Premium version only):** Select discount coupon type sent as a reward for subscription.
* **Discount Coupon Amount (Premium version only):** Set discount coupon value.
* **Expires In (Premium version only):** Set coupon expiry duration for coupon sent during subscription.
* **From Email:** Email address used for email headers from which the welcome email & newsletter will be sent.
* **Reply To Email:** Email address used for email headers to which the subscribers can reach back to us when replying to the email they received
* **Subscription Details:** Details about the newsletter subscription service that you want to inform your visitors. This will be visible on the WooCommerce My Account page registration form, Checkout form and Account Details form.

== Installation ==

1. Go to the Add New plugins screen in your WordPress admin area
2. Click the upload tab
3. Browse for the plugin file (wl-wc-newsletter.zip)
4. Click Install Now and then activate the plugin
5. Now, go to the settings menu of the plugin and follow the instructions
